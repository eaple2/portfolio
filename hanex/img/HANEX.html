                  Inspired by the Splendor of Nature

                  Stratum series is inspired by nature in a worldly perspective and is full filled with a soft range of colors and unique, organic patterns.
                  You discover the artistic,unconventional scene of the nature. Enjoy the new view of the earth.
                  Create your own, unique and long-lasting atmosphere in the style of the depth andboundlessness of stone.

                  view more
                  Inspired by the Splendor of Nature

                  Discover the greatness and glory of Bellassimo collection which is inspired by the beauty of the night sky and twilight. The vein expresses the colorful beauty of the sky, and the chip decorates the starlight of the night sky.

                  view more
                  Inspired by the Splendor of Nature

                  Inspired by the natural beauty of cascading waterfalls. Cascade collection features delicate veining and subtle variation that perfectly resemble the effortless flow of water. Its low color contrast trait allows Hanex to be matched with any interior space.

                  view more
                  Inspired by the Splendor of Nature

                  Venato is a series of Hanex into the design and manufacture of solid surfaces which are not merely imitations of the real marble. After having reinterpreted the texture and vein of calacatta, Hanex now offers a new way of considering the preciousness of a noble marble.
